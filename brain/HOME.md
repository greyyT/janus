# Janus Brain

Janus is a Markdown-first personal engineering knowledge system.

## Current v0 model

- Root Markdown files are capture inbox notes unless they are protected default files.
- `brain/` is durable wiki memory.
- Root inbox notes may be incomplete, incorrect, or unverified.
- Promoting a root note into `brain/` is the deliberate act of preserving it as engineering memory.
