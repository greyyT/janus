# Janus Brain

Janus is a Markdown-first personal engineering knowledge system.

## Current v0.3 model

- `journal/` contains dated daily notes (`journal/YYYY-MM-DD.md`) for chronological daily working memory, morning check-ins, committed task blocks, notes, and checkout reflections.
- `backlog.md` is the protected root queue of unresolved, uncommitted tasks.
- `brain/` is durable wiki memory.
- Root inbox notes may be incomplete, incorrect, or unverified.
- Promoting a root note into `brain/` is the deliberate act of preserving it as durable engineering memory.
